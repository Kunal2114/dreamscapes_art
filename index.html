Studio overview

Dreamscapes Art Studio is a dynamic creative space serving as a collaborative platform for exceptionally talented artists and craftsmen from across India, bringing together diverse art forms under one vision of creativity and community engagement.

With expertise spanning handicrafts, paintings, murals, metal casting, wood carving, sculptures, installations, and contemporary design, the studio blends traditional craftsmanship with modern artistic perspectives — inspiring imagination and creating immersive experiences that resonate with people from all walks of life.

Our vision

A creative and collaborative art space established in 2025 in New Delhi, dedicated to promoting artistic expression by bringing together talented artists and craftsmen from across India — blending traditional craftsmanship with modern creativity while inspiring and engaging the community through art.

Who we work with

Art lovers, collectors, interior designers, architects, corporate clients, and cultural organizations seeking unique and meaningful artistic creations — as well as students, emerging artists, and creative individuals who value craftsmanship and cultural artistry.
